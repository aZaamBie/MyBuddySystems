App Features

A.I. Helper buddy

Upon registration, users fill out a form asking simple questions about their background, visa status, and needed information. With this data, the MyBuddy app generates a personalized, A.I-driven virtual companion that provides users with the exact information they need for their stay in Munich. With the help of a keyword feature, users can easily select or enter keywords to inform their buddy on the needed information: By utilizing these keywords and the user profile, the virtual buddy promptly retrieves essential information from government websites and offers users a personalized, simplified guide tailored to their preferred language and literacy level.
Apart from questions regarding comprehensive newcomer data, users can also pose various other inquiries to their Buddy concerning their Munich stay, such as "Where is the nearest Aldi?" or "How do I reach Maximilianstrasse?" - similar to a personal guide and friend, The AI Buddy adeptly supplies all requisite information to users,within seconds.


Disclaimer: the AI buddy will not engage in any personal conversation with the user and only answer factual questions about the city of Munich – thus ensuring that the MyBuddy AI buddy does not in any way become a replacement for human interaction.

Buddy-to-Buddy connections

An algorithm groups users with similar backgrounds or requirements and suggests them to each other, encouraging them to connect. When users pose questions to their buddy, they have the option to share these inquiries with their connected buddies, who may share a similar circumstance or have undergone comparable procedures. Rather than solely relying on administrative personnel to address inquiries, users can initially consult others in analogous situations and exchange city-related information, fostering a cooperative environment among like-minded users. Should users still desire to speak to an official city representative to address their inquiries, they can request to share their questions with admin staff profiles. These staff members can then respond to their queries through a chatbox and offer personalized, one-to-one assistance.


Disclaimer: The users personal buddy will filter out any misleading information or potential spam. Users can only chat about information regarding their stay in Munich: any personal chats will be removed immediately.

The Map Feature

The MyBuddy App includes a full map of Munich with all the street names and the user’s live location. This map also includes pop-up icon recommendations of restaurants, hotels, shops and cultural places which can be adjusted to the users preferred price range.


Cultural Preference Setting

Users are able to select the culture that they most identify within on the MyBuddy app. MyBuddy will then notify the user about events happening around Munich that are connected to this culture. The map feature will also include pop-up icons of cultural shops and restaurants as well as the location of said cultural events currently taking place.
