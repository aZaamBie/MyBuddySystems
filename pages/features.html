App Features

A.I. Helper buddy

Upon registration, users fill out a form asking simple questions about their background, visa status, and needed information. With this data, the MyBuddy app generates a personalized, A.I-driven virtual companion that provides users with the exact information they need for their stay in Munich. With the help of a keyword feature, users can easily select or enter keywords to inform their buddy on the needed information: By utilizing these keywords and the user profile, the virtual buddy promptly retrieves essential information from government websites and offers users a personalized, simplified guide tailored to their preferred language and literacy level.
Apart from questions regarding comprehensive newcomer data, users can also pose various other inquiries to their Buddy concerning their Munich stay, such as "Where is the nearest Aldi?" or "How do I reach Maximilianstrasse?" - similar to a personal guide and friend, The AI Buddy adeptly supplies all requisite information to users,within seconds.


Disclaimer: the AI buddy will not engage in any personal conversation with the user and only answer factual questions about the city of Munich – thus ensuring that the MyBuddy AI buddy does not in any way become a replacement for human interaction.

Welcome Checklist

Once the buddy is generated, users can select specific goals for their time in Munich, such as "Applying for a resident visa" or "Exploring work options". Based on this input, the buddy creates a personalized checklist for the user, offering clear guidance for each objective

Within each checkpoint, users can access a comprehensive FAQ section and reach out to their buddy for assistance whenever needed. Should users still require further support, they have the option to submit their query to an appropriate government representative office and schedule a call. Representatives can review users' concerns in advance, ensuring requests are directed to the correct department and enabling them to prepare beforehand. Ultimately, this fosters efficient, personalized support for each newcomer

As users progress through their checklist, they earn rewards for completing tasks such as upgrading their buddy with additional features or receiving discounts from our partner businesses in Munich, including local supermarkets and shops. This strategy provides mutual advantages: On one hand, it boosts user engagement, turning the newcomer journey into an enjoyable and interactive experience rather than a tedious one. On the other, it enables MyBuddy to partner with local businesses keen on presenting their offerings to newcomers, thereby generating essential revenue.

Optional Feature for Future Implementation

The Map Feature

Currently, the primary focus of the MyBuddy app is to offer users personalized information through their virtual buddy and checklist. However, in the future, additional features can be incorporated to acquaint users with their immediate surroundings and assist them in other aspects, for example in finding jobs that match their specific qualifications.


One such feature could be the "Map Feature." This feature provides a comprehensive map of Munich, displaying all street names and the user's real-time location. It also includes pop-up icon recommendations for restaurants, hotels, shops, and cultural sites, which can be tailored to the user's cultural preferences and budget. Additionally, users can explore businesses nearby that offer jobs aligned with their qualifications and job preferences. When users express interest in a job, they are directed to the corresponding application form on the business' website. This feature would offer new residents a convenient map overview of key locations in the city and relevant information about nearby opportunities.
