MyBuddy Systems – what it’s all about

MyBuddy is an app designed not only to familiarize users with the beautiful city of Munich, which is rich in culture and experiences, but to also connect them to suitable locals. It allows those who do not yet know Munich to safely explore and fulfil their lust for adventure within the city - with the use of their own, virtual friend.

MyBuddy caters not only to the direct needs of newcomers to Munich but is also designed for locals and frequent visitors. With the help of a virtual buddy, users effortlessly access vital city information and connect with like-minded individuals to seek advice and address concerns - Whether it's navigating essential newcomer procedures like visa applications, employment inquiries, or staying updated on the latest Munich events: MyBuddy offers comprehensive support, thus fostering an accessible, secure and collaborative environment for newcomers and established residents alike.
